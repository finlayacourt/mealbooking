Seated catering provision, capacities and ventilation:

From 11 January 2022 takeaway provision will cease and seated dining will resume. Please exercise caution in wearing face coverings whilst entering and queuing for food. In Hall student and staff tables at lunch will be separated.

All dining venues, including the Bar, will re-open for Lent Term. No reduction in capacity due to Covid is planned. Ventilation will be actively managed through open doors and windows, managed by staff (please speak to a staff member before making changes), and hence warm clothing is advised!


Thank you
Jennifer Phillips (Domestic Bursar)
